Course Schedule and Readings

While the topics covered are broad, each week will feature different examples from genetics, ecology, molecular, and evolutionary biology highlighting uses of each individual set of techniques. Each topic will have accompanying readings highlighting a general introduction to a technique (required), and one or more general references or examples. For access to blocked readings, biol609.

To see the code of a lecture, load the slides, then change the file extension in your browser bar to .Rmd to get the code. Or go to http://github.com/biol609/biol609.github.io/lectures for all of the code.

Week 1.

Lecture: Why Advanced Data Analysis?, GLM Review
Etherpad: https://etherpad.wikimedia.org/p/609-intro
Reading: O’Hara 2009 through section on GLMs, O’Hara and Kotze 2010, Wharton and Hui 2011, ver Hoef and Boveng 2007 on overdispersion, Hartig DHARMa vignette
Files: Data for GLM Lecture
